Hosting and domain services are integral components of establishing an online presence. Web hosting refers to the storage and maintenance of your website's files and data on a server, allowing it to be accessible on the internet. Hosting providers offer various plans to suit different needs, from shared hosting for smaller websites to dedicated hosting for high-traffic or resource-intensive sites.

Domains, on the other hand, serve as the unique address for your website, making it easily accessible to users. A domain name, such as "www.yourwebsite.com," is a critical part of your online identity. Domain registrars assist in purchasing and managing domain names.
